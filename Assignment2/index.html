KickStarter Goals from 2014 to 2017

When focusing on the countries to the left, we can see that four out of the five countries have had the average project funding goal increase significantly. On the other hand, the average project goal for United States has actually decreased from 2014 to 2017. Nonetheless, it is important to note, the average project goal is still much higher for Unitef States as compared to New Zealand and United Kingdom.

The status KickStarter Projects per Launch Month

The month during which the KickStarter project is launched can impact whether or not it is successful in reaching and/or surpassing its monetary goal. Firstly, the line chart on the left indicates, that overall, the number of suspended projects isn't impacted by the launch month. The successful and failed KickStarter projects follow the same trend. Between 2009 and 2017, projects launched between June to August, have succeeded and failed the most. Overall, there are more failures each month, than successful projects.
